Apple Crisp

Description

This easy apple crisp has a topping of oats, brown sugar, cinnimon, and butter.

Ingredients
  • Apples
  • Brown sugar
  • Quick oats
  • Flour
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Cut 3-4 med. apples
  2. Combine
    • 3/4 Cup brown sugar
    • 3/4 Cup quick oats
    • 1/ Cup flour
  3. Cut w/ 1/2 cup butter
  4. Sprinkle over apples
  5. Bake @ 350Â°F for 35-40 minutes
